About Lviv:

Lviv is the largest city in Western Ukraine, and the sixth-largest in Ukraine, with a population of 717,510 (2021 est.)[6] Lviv is one of the main cultural centres of Ukraine. It was named in honour of Leo, the eldest son of Daniel, King of Ruthenia.
