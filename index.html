About country:
"is a country in Eastern Europe. It is the second-largest European country, covering approximately 600,000 square kilometres (230,000 sq mi), and has a population of around 40 million people"

It is bordered by russia to the east and northeast by belarus to the north by Poland, Slovakia, and Hungary to the west; and by Romania and Moldova to the southwest; with a coastline along the Black Sea and the Sea of Azov to the south and southeast, respectively. Kyiv is Ukraine's capital and largest city. The country's language is Ukrainian.

Why should travel to Ukraine?

Ukraine offers great pastime all-the-year-round, for every taste and budget. You can explore the remote Carpathian Mountains, as well as bustling city life; discover a medieval cultural heritage or find out more about Soviet architecture; marvel at the beauty of Kiev or visit an abandoned Chornobyl zone.
